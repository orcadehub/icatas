Scope of the Conference

Scope:

Sustainable development is the idea that human societies must live and meet their needs without compromising the ability of future generations. It ensures a balance between present and future needs, considering environmental preservation, natural resources, social, and economic equity.

ICATAS’25 focuses on innovative technologies and applications for sustainable engineering, science, society, energy, material sciences, and the environment.
